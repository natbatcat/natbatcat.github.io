MOS

Mos was found trying to cross a busy urban street. His rescuer pulled over, psp psp psp'ed at him, and Mos hopped right into his car, immediately snuggling up to his small chihuahua mix in the back seat. Mos is incredibly friendly- he loves everyone. He came to us underweight but judging by his temperment and manners he was once a house cat who may have gotten out and gotten lost. He is thankful to have been rescued and waiting patiently for his forever family to take him home.
